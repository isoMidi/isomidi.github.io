Privacy Policy

Effective date: September 18, 2025

StoryTime Kids – FairyLand (“the App”) is developed and operated by vEdu (“we,” “us,” or “our”). We are committed to protecting children’s privacy and complying with applicable laws and platform policies, including the App Store Kids Category requirements and COPPA where applicable.

1) What We Collect

We designed the App to minimize data collection. We do not collect personal contact information such as a child’s name, address, phone number, or precise location. The App uses Firebase Analytics Without Ad ID Support to collect limited, anonymous information to help us improve the App:

  • App usage events (e.g., app opens, screens viewed, generic interactions)
  • App instance identifiers (random, resettable identifiers created by Firebase—not advertising identifiers)
  • Diagnostics such as crash and performance data (only if the related Firebase services are enabled)

We do not collect IDFA/Advertising ID, precise geolocation, health data, financial data, or other sensitive categories.

2) How We Use Data

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the App’s quality, stability, and performance
  • To understand general usage patterns (in aggregate) and enhance the user experience

We do not use data for behavioral advertising, profiling, or cross-app tracking.

3) Data Sharing & Service Providers

We may share limited, anonymous data with our service provider Google Firebase to deliver analytics and diagnostics. Data is used strictly on our behalf and according to their privacy and security practices. We do not sell personal data.

4) Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for young children and is designed to comply with applicable children’s privacy laws. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us immediately so we can delete it.

5) Parental Rights & Controls

Parents and legal guardians may contact us to review or request deletion of any information associated with their child’s use of the App. Because we only collect anonymous analytics and diagnostics, we may not be able to identify individual users; however, we will honor requests to the extent technically feasible.

6) Data Retention

We retain analytics and diagnostic data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Policy, after which it is deleted or de-identified according to our and Firebase’s retention practices.

7) Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we continually work to improve our safeguards.

8) International Transfers

Depending on your location, data may be processed in countries with privacy laws different from yours. We rely on service providers that implement appropriate safeguards consistent with applicable legal requirements.
